Authors and Affiliations

The Grebe Social Media Aggregator is developed by Hamman Samuel, PhD Candidate at the Department of Computing Science, University of Alberta, under the supervision of Dr. Osmar Zaiane, Scientific Director of the Alberta Machine Intelligence Institute, Amii.

On-going Development

Currently, Grebe aggregates geo-fenced Twitter data from provinces in Canada, including Alberta, British Columbia, Saskatchewan, and Manitoba. We have a repository of over 2.5 million tweets (and counting) since July 2016. We are continuously expanding our platform to support new features, as well as aggregating social data from other provinces in Canada and other social data platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, and Snapchat.

Fun Facts

Grebe was initiated in July 2016 with focus on aggregating tweets from the province of Alberta. The name Grebe is taken from the horned grebe, a native Albertan bird.
